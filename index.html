Products

    We take pride in our ability to make any piece from any material, taking care to make sure the final result is functional and aesthetically pleasing. We specialize in toolworking, but we also forge and mend blades and shears, as well as larger furnishings and decorations.

Classes

    On Tuesdays and Thursdays we invite groups of people to come learn from our smiths, both by watching them at work and by taking lessons. We offer coaching in basic forging, building your own forge, maintaing a forge and tools, basic hammering and metalworking skills, and more! We gaurantee the best one-on-one teaching in the state. Occasionally we take apprentices, usually from among those we've taught some basic lessons to. Come learn the art of blacksmithing!

About Us

    Redford blacksmithing is a local, family-owned workshop based in Burbeon, Tennessee. We've been in business for over 50 years, passing our trade from father to son, and we aim to set the standard for quality, whatever the piece. We currently have 3 Master blacksmiths: Mark, Andy, and John, with Nathan doing most of our welding. We also have 4 Tradesmen, and 3 Apprentice blacksmiths.
    Recently we've been updating and renovating our main shop, and we plan on adding a third building where we can do most of our welding, separate from the main forge. We love visitors, and are more than happy to show you around!
